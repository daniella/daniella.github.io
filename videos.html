Videos

Below are video journalism projects I have created for some of my classes. Feel free to look at some of my other video clips and exercises here.

Ken Rembert is the owner of The Bagel Bakery in Gainesville, Florida. His restaurant serves a variety of breakfast and lunch items made on site every day. In this video, shot on April 12, 2013, Rembert explains the steps Nick Makris and Carl Renshaw, The Bagel Bakery employees, follow in order to make the bagels at his restaurant. Makris starts by preparing the dough used for the bagels and later places pieces of the dough in a bagel-forming machine. Renshaw then puts those bagels in the oven and keeps an eye on them so that they don't burn.

Margaret Wagener is the Volunteer Coordinator for the book house volunteers of the Friends of the Library Book Sale. In this video, shot on April 2013, Wagener explains how the now biannual book sale got its start and operates.
